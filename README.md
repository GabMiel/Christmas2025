### My Christmas Message Project

This is my Christmas 2025 website and I made it to help people find their special Christmas message. I wanted it to feel happy and magical because Christmas is my favorite time of the year.

When you type your name, the button makes golden sparkles that fly everywhere because I wanted it to look like Christmas magic. After that, it brings you to the next page where you can choose what kind of message you want to read.

There is snow falling in the background and everything looks bright and shiny because Christmas lights always make me feel warm and excited. I hope people smile when they use this project because it is made for sharing joy with everyone.

Merry Christmas and thank you for visiting my project.
